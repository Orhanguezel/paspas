FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer OEM manufacturing?+

Yes. Promats offers OEM manufacturing support for B2B customers, distributors and automotive brands. Contact our export team to discuss your specific requirements.

Do you provide private label services?+

Yes. Private label options may include customer logo application, product labeling, barcode labeling and custom packaging depending on order quantity.

Can I receive samples before placing a bulk order?+

Yes. Sample shipment is available for qualified B2B buyers. Contact us to discuss your product interest and we will arrange accordingly.

What is your minimum order quantity?+

MOQ depends on the product series selected and whether the order includes packaging or branding requirements. Please contact us directly for an accurate minimum quantity for your specific needs.

Which countries do you currently export to?+

Promats currently exports to more than 35 countries. Our export experience covers Europe, the Middle East, Central Asia, Africa and beyond. Contact us to confirm availability in your market.

What are your available shipping and incoterm options?+

We support sea, road and air freight depending on destination and order volume. EXW and FOB are our standard incoterms, but other arrangements can be discussed at the quotation stage.

How long does production take?+

Production lead time is confirmed at the order stage and depends on order volume, product series and any specific branding or packaging requirements included in the order.
